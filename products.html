Umbrella Policy

Extra protection above your limits

An umbrella policy provides an extra layer of liability coverage that kicks in when your auto or home policy limits are exhausted. It's one of the best values in insurance — and most people skip it.

  • $1M–$5M+ in additional liability coverage
  • Covers auto and home liability above policy limits
  • Protects assets in lawsuits
  • Covers incidents your other policies may miss
  • Affordable — often $150–$300/year
  • Ideal for homeowners, business owners, and parents
